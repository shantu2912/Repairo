We Protect
Your Privacy.

At FixZen, trust is our foundation. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you invite our experts into your home.

We collect only what is necessary to serve you:

  • Identity: Name, Profile Photo.
  • Contact: Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Location: Precise GPS location to match you with nearby technicians.
  • Home Details: Addresses saved for service delivery.

• Service Matching: To find the closest available expert for your specific problem.

• Safety: To track the technician's journey to your home for your safety.

• Communication: To send booking confirmations, invoices, and technician arrival updates.

We DO NOT sell your personal data.

We share data strictly with:

1. The Technician: Only your first name and location, 1 hour before the job starts.

2. Payment Gateways: Encrypted transaction data for processing payments.

3. Legal Authorities: Only if required by Indian law.

• All data is encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption.

• Technician phone numbers are masked; they cannot see your real number, only call through the app.

• We conduct mandatory background verification on all partners before they join the platform.

You have the right to:

• Request a copy of all data we hold about you.

• Request deletion of your account and data ("Right to be Forgotten").

• Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
